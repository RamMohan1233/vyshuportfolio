Info

The purpose of E-Medicare System is to automate the existing manual system by the help of computerized equipment's and full-fledged computer software, fulfilling their requirements, so that their valuable data/information can be stored for a longer period with easy accessing and manipulation of the same.The “Online Medical Store” has been developed to override the problems prevailing in the practicing manual system. This software is supposed to eliminate and reduce the hardships faced by the existing system. Online Medical Store can lead to error free, secure, reliable and fast management system.
